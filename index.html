About Us

Welcome to PrintScrapHub, your trusted partner in printer cartridge solutions and metal scrap trading. With over 15 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a leading supplier of high-quality printer cartridges and printing supplies and a reliable importer, trader of non-ferrous metal scraps in India.

Our Expertise in Printer Cartridges

For over a decade and a half, we have been at the forefront of the printer cartridge industry, offering an extensive range of products sourced from Europe, the UK, China, Japan, and other global markets. Our network includes some of the most reputed vendors and suppliers, allowing us to provide top-quality empty, used, OEM inkjet and toner cartridges and recycled printer cartridges at competitive prices. Whether you are a reseller, distributor, or corporate buyer, we ensure quick deliveries, best pricing, and seamless logistics.

Metal Scrap Trading in India

Expanding our portfolio, we are also engaged in the procurement and distribution of metal scraps across India. We specialize in non-ferrous scraps like zorba, zurik, aluminum, copper, and brass used in various industries. Our strong partnerships with global scrap yards and recyclers help us offer a consistent supply of premium-grade materials to meet the demands of Indian buyers.
