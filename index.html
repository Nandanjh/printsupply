Metal Scrap

Non-Ferrous Metal Scraps – Zorba & Zurik from Japan

We specialize in sourcing high-quality Zorba and Zurik non-ferrous metal scraps from leading recycling companies in Japan, ensuring reliable supply and premium-grade materials for industrial applications. Currently handling 300MT annually, we cater to buyers looking for consistent, clean, and well-sorted non-ferrous scrap for recycling and reuse.

Product Details:

✅ Zorba: A mix of shredded non-ferrous metals, primarily aluminum, along with traces of copper, brass, zinc, and stainless steel, recovered from automobile and electronic scrap. Ideal for refining and smelting.
✅ Zurik: A blend of stainless steel and other non-ferrous metals, including copper, brass, and zinc, offering a high-grade scrap solution for secondary metal processing industries.

Why Choose Our Japanese Sourced Scrap?

✅ High Purity & Quality: Sourced from well-established Japanese recyclers with stringent sorting processes.
✅ Consistent Supply: Handling 300MT annually, ensuring a steady flow of material.
✅Competitive Pricing: Direct procurement from Japan with efficient logistics solutions.
✅ Sustainable & Eco-Friendly: Promoting circular economy by recycling valuable metal resources.

Saisei: A New Life from the Old

In Japanese, "Saisei" (再生) means rebirth, regeneration, and revival—a philosophy deeply rooted in the idea of giving new life to what was once discarded. It symbolizes not just renewal, but a commitment to sustainability, transformation, and innovation.

At Saisei Global, we embody this spirit by turning waste into opportunity, transforming discarded materials into valuable resources. Just as nature follows the cycle of rebirth, we believe that nothing truly ends—it simply takes a new form. Our mission is to reduce environmental impact, minimize waste, and create a circular economy where every resource finds a second life.

Our mission is to ensure that metals, plastics, electronics, and industrial waste don’t just fade into oblivion but are reborn into something useful and purposeful. With Saisei, waste is not the end—it’s just the beginning of something new.
